Stair Traversing Dolly

Project Overview

Identified a user need through multiple interviews and developed a solution to address the problem. Designed a stair-traversing dolly that can be pulled up stairs with ease and prevents rollback when left unattended. The project involved ideation, prototyping, and testing to ensure the dolly meets user safety and usability standards.

One-Way Motion Mechanism

The dolly incorporates a ratchet mechanism on its wheels, allowing movement only in the upward direction when traversing stairs. This prevents the dolly from rolling backward and ensures safety when left unattended on stairs.

Prototype Testing

The prototype underwent rigorous testing to evaluate its stability, load capacity, and effectiveness in stair traversal. Results confirmed:

  • Safe and smooth operation when ascending stairs.
  • Automatic braking prevents rollback.
  • Lightweight yet sturdy design for easy transport.
